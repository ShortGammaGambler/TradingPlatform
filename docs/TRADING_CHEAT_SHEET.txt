📊 SIGNAL COLORS & MEANINGS
====================================

WHITE ARROWS (Primary Signals)
  ↑ White Arrow Up = HIGH PROBABILITY LONG
  ↓ White Arrow Down = HIGH PROBABILITY SHORT
  → Wait for these! (5+ confirmations)

CYAN/MAGENTA ARROWS (Liquidity)
  ↑ Cyan Arrow = Bullish Liquidity Sweep
  ↓ Magenta Arrow = Bearish Liquidity Sweep
  → Stop hunt detected, expect reversal

GREEN/RED TRIANGLES (Divergence)
  △ Green Triangle = Bullish Divergence
  ▽ Red Triangle = Bearish Divergence
  → Price/volume disagreement

LIGHT GREEN/PINK ARROWS (Structure)
  ↑ Light Green = Bullish Market Structure Break
  ↓ Light Pink = Bearish Market Structure Break
  → Trend continuation signal

DARK GREEN/RED SQUARES (Extremes)
  ■ Dark Green = Oversold Zone (reversal area)
  ■ Dark Red = Overbought Zone (reversal area)
  → High probability mean reversion

📈 INDICATOR LINES
====================================

GREEN LINE = Order Block Support
  → Institutional buying zone, expect bounce

RED LINE = Order Block Resistance
  → Institutional selling zone, expect rejection

ORANGE LINE = Volume POC (Point of Control)
  → High-volume price level, acts as magnet

YELLOW LINE = Cumulative Delta
  → Green = buyers in control
  → Red = sellers in control

WHITE LINE (VWAP in Scalper)
  → Institutional benchmark
  → Above = bullish, Below = bearish
