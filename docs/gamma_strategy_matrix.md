# Gamma Scanner Strategy Matrix

## Scanner Score → Strategy Selection

### Score 12+ (Exceptional - 80% Win Rate Expected)
**Setup**: Perfect storm - volume spike + gamma level + institutional hours + momentum
**Max Position Size**: 2-3% of account

### Score 10-11 (Excellent - 70% Win Rate Expected)  
**Setup**: Strong confluence - multiple factors aligned
**Standard Position Size**: 1-2% of account

### Score 8-9 (Good - 60% Win Rate Expected)
**Setup**: Decent setup - limited risk
**Conservative Size**: 0.5-1% of account

## Strike Selection by Setup Type

### Type 1: Gamma Pin Play (Stock Approaching Round Number)
**Example**: AAPL at $148.70, targeting $150.00

**Call Strategy (Bullish Pin)**:
- **Primary**: Buy $150 calls (ATM at target)
- **Alternative**: Buy $149 calls (slight ITM for higher delta)
- **Conservative**: $148 calls (deeper ITM, lower risk/reward)

**Put Strategy (Bearish Pin)**:
- **Primary**: Buy $150 puts if rejecting the level
- **Alternative**: $151 puts if showing weakness above

**Time Frame**: 0-1 DTE for maximum gamma exposure

### Type 2: Breakout Above Round Number
**Example**: AAPL breaking above $150.00 with volume

**Call Strategies**:
- **Aggressive**: $151 calls (betting on continuation)
- **Balanced**: $150 calls (already ITM, riding momentum)
- **Conservative**: $149 calls (safer delta, less theta risk)

**Entry**: Wait for 1-minute close above round number + volume

### Type 3: Rejection at Round Number  
**Example**: AAPL hitting $150.00 but showing weakness

**Put Strategies**:
- **Aggressive**: $150 puts (betting on rejection)
- **Balanced**: $149 puts (target below round number)
- **Conservative**: $148 puts (further pullback target)

**Entry**: Look for reversal candle + volume at the round number
